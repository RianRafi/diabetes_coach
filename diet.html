Benefits and Calorie Counts

Breakfast: A balanced breakfast can kickstart your metabolism, provide essential nutrients, and improve concentration and performance throughout the day. Typical calorie count: 300-400 calories.

Lunch: A nutritious lunch helps to maintain energy levels and prevent overeating in the evening. It is important for sustained productivity and mood stabilization. Typical calorie count: 400-600 calories.

Dinner: A healthy dinner can support weight management, improve digestion, and promote better sleep. It should be lighter compared to lunch to avoid discomfort. Typical calorie count: 400-600 calories.

Snacks: Healthy snacks can keep your energy up and prevent overeating during meals. They should be nutrient-dense rather than calorie-dense. Typical calorie count: 100-200 calories per snack.
